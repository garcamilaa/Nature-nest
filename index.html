About Nature Nest

Nature Nest History

Since 2024, a group of friends passionate about gardening and sustainability came together to bring an innovative idea to life: to create a planter that was not only functional and aesthetically appealing, but was also environmentally friendly. After months of research and experimentation, they finally found the perfect combination of organic and biodegradable materials for the pot, along with a compost-based fertilizer that would be integrated into the walls of the pot.

About our product

SDG

Our project consists of goal 15 “life on land” in which we want to focus on taking care of our environment Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, and halt and reverse land degradation and halt biodiversity loss.

Process

First we get cardboard, then we crush it into pieces, and we mix it with water to put it around the silicon mold, then we did the compost that is made of dirt and vegetable/ fruit peel, then we put the compost in the mold, and finally we let it dry and add the fertilizer.
